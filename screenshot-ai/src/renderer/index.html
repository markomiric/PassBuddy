Quick Start

Available Hotkeys:

  • Ctrl+Shift+S - Capture a screenshot
  • Ctrl+Alt+S - Toggle window visibility
  • Ctrl+Shift+M - Toggle voice recording
  • Ctrl+Alt+R - Reset application
  • Alt+D - Toggle dark/light mode

The app will extract text from your screenshot or voice recording and analyze it with AI
